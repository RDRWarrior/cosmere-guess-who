Rules
  • Host starts first.
  • You must only use yes or no questions to narrow down your opponent's cognitive aspect.
  • Use 'Cross Out' to remove characters from your own board and use 'Restore' if you crossed someone out by mistake.
  • When your turn is over, you must click 'end turn'.
  • A note section is at the bottom of the page to help you remember what questions you have asked.
  • You may NOT ask a question and then guess. A guess must be a turn. But be warned, if you guess wrong, you lose.
  • Spectators can watch the game but cannot make moves.
  • Any character referenced in a deck's book series may be in said deck - ie, Cultivation mentioned in Secret History allows her to be in the Mistborn deck.
  • A character's name may be different depending on which deck that card was taken from - ie, Thaidakar. If they are in two decks you have picked, it will be randomized on which is grabbed.
  • Spoilers may be hard to avoid, please keep that in mind if playing and not fully Cosmerically aware.
  • If you do not know who a character is, click their name for a hint!
  • Most importantly, HAVE FUN :D
